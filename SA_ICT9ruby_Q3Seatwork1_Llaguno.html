Avatar the Last Airbender

Synopsis :

The were four countries based on the different elements. The Earth Kingdom, The Water tribes of the South and North Pole, The Airnomads and thier 4 great temples and the Fire nation. All was well until the Fire Nation attacked. 100 years later there is still no end in sight. Meanwhile two siblings from the water tribe. Sokka and his waterbender sister Katara discover the avatar Aang who can master all four elements and is they key to restoring peace to the world.

Characters :

Aang
He is the avatar a being blessed by the spirits of the spirit realm. Every generation has its own avatar to keep/ restore peace. Aang is the current avatar and he must learn how to master the three other elements.
Sokka
He is a scrwany pragmatic fighter. What he lacks in bending and martial prowess he makes up for in sheer inginueity being the brains of all of the strategies of the team.
Katara
She is the sister of sokka. She is a caring young girl who discovers she is the onyl remaining waterbender of the southern Water tribe.She despereatly wants to escape to go to the Northern water tribe to learn more on her waterbending skills.
Toph biefong
Hailing from a rich family toph is a girl whoose extreme toughness is usually underestimated due to her less threatning appearance and her being blind.
Prince Zuko
The Disgraced son of the Fire Lord he is sent to do the impossible wich is to locate and kill the Avatar. Fortunately for him he is accompanied by a ship with his Uncle Iroh.
The Fire Lord Ozai
He continues the war after the previous Fire lord died. He plans on using a metoer that comes once every 100 years as a power boost to end the war and establish Fire Nation dominance across all the lands.
